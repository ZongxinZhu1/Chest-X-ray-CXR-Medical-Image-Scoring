Load the manifest first. Binary preference and scoring are optional, but at least one action is required.
Scoring guideline
2 points: The image is a perfect match to the report, and all pathologies and details are accurately represented.
1 point: The image reflects most of the pathologies and details mentioned in the report, with no major error.
0 point: The image reflects only the core pathology in the report, while some details are missing.
-1 point: The image fails to reflect all pathologies mentioned in the report and shows obvious omissions.
-2 point: The image is completely mismatched with the report, with no corresponding pathology or with serious errors.
Please assign a score directly for each sample set.
